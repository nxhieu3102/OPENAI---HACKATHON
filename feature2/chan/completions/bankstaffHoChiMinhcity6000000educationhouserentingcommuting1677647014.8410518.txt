Education:
For education, allocate 30% of your budget to drawing and cooking lessons. Drawing classes can consist of online classes or physical classes, while cooking classes can consist of online tutorials or in-person classes. Consider the prices of each type of class and look for discounts or deals to maximize your budget.

House Rental:
For house rental, allocate 50% of your budget to renting a house in a suburban area. Research the average prices for different types of houses in the area and look for discounts or deals that could help you save money.

Commuting:
For commuting, allocate 20% of your budget to transportation expenses. Consider the cost of buying and maintaining a motorbike, as well as the cost of fuel. Look for discounted offers on motorbike rentals or public transportation if available. Also, make sure to factor in the cost of any necessary repairs or maintenance.
