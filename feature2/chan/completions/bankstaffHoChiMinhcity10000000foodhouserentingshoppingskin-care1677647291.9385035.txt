Food: Allocate 30% of the budget for food. Focus on buying high protein foods from local markets, as these are usually cheaper than supermarket. Consider buying in bulk to save money. 

House Renting: Allocate 40% of the budget for house renting. Consider getting a cheap motel to stay in temporarily. This is more cost-efficient than renting a permanent apartment and it allows more flexibility in the long-term. 

Shopping: Allocate 15% of the budget for shopping. Consider buying items online to save money. Use coupon codes and discounts whenever possible. 

Skin-care: Allocate 15% of the budget for skin-care. Consider buying products from drugstores and discount stores. Research the ingredients of products and select ones that suit your skin type.
