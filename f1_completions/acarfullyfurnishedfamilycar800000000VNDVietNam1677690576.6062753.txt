1. Honda Civic: This is perhaps the most cost-effective choice for the given budget of 800,000,000 VND. The Civic will easily fit the needs of a fully furnished family car, with room for up to 5 passengers, lots of trunk space, and a plethora of comfort and connectivity features. It's well-rounded engine is responsive and powerful, offering solid performance for its price point. Plus, it's reliable, economical and attractive. All in all, it's a great car for the price. Price: 756,000,000 VND.

2. Mazda3: If you're looking for something sportier, the Mazda3 is a great choice. It may be slightly over budget, but the driving experience and the sleek modern design of bodywork makes it worth the extra money. It seats five passengers, with ample space in the rear, and is powered by a responsive and efficient engine. Connectivity features are plentiful, and the car looks great on the road. It delivers an experience of luxury within the given budget. Price: 799,000,000 VND.

3. Ford Focus: The Ford Focus is another great option for the given budget. It gives you a light, nimble performance that is great for commuting to and from work, while still giving you the convenience of a full-size car. It also has plenty of room for passengers, ample trunk space and a laundry list of convenience features. Plus, it's affordable for the budget. Price: 739,000,000 VND.
