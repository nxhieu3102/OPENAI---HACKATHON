Ford Escape (Price: 478,000,000 VND):
The Ford Escape is an attractive choice, especially for the price. It offers a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine coupled with a 6 speed automatic transmission. Safety is also taken care of, with the presence of multiple airbags, an anti-lock braking system, and other features that maximize occupant safety. It also comes with a 2-year/50,000 km warranty and roadside assistance.
