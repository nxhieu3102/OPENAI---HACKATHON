1. Honda Civic Type R (Fk8) - 599.000.000 VND
The Honda Civic Type R is one of the most popular sports car in the world and is often praised for its performance and enjoyment. The type R version of Honda Civic comes with a 2.0L turbocharged engine producing 300 horsepower and 400 Nm of torque, mated to a 6-speed manual transmission. This combination allows you to reach a top speed of 270 km/h and accelerate from 0-100 km/h in just 5.7 seconds. Additionally, this car comes standard with most goodies like Push Start, leather/Alcantara interior, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto, and more. 
 
2. BMW M2 - 595.000.000 VND
The BMW M2 is a highly acclaimed performance sedan from the elite M model lineup from the German automaker. The car features a 3.0L inline-6 engine, mated to BMW's signature 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. It offers swift acceleration, reaching 0-100 km/h in 4.3 seconds, with a top speed of 250 km/h. The M2 also offers an assortment of luxuries standard, such as engine start-stop button, climate control, heated seats and more.
 
3. Volkswagen Golf R - 570.000.000 VND
The VW Golf R is a classic performance hatchback with a 2.0L turbocharged engine, mated to a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. It produces 300 horsepower and 380 Nm of torque, while reaching 0-100 km/h in 5.1 seconds. The Golf R also comes with some standard features, such as dualzone climate control, panoramic sunroof, adaptive dampers, and more. Additionally, its cabin has plenty of room for your family and friends, making it perfect for a daily driver. 
 
These are three excellent choices for a sports car within the 600000000 VND budget. No matter which one you choose, you will be getting an amazing car with plenty of performance and luxury features. The Honda Civic Type R is probably the best choice for performance seekers, while the BMW M2 is great for those looking for luxury features. The VW Golf R is the perfect balance of performance and luxury.
