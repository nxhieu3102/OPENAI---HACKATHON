Toyota Yaris: This is a great option for those looking for a fuel-efficient car in Vietnam. It has a 1.5-liter engine and has a fuel consumption rate of 5.7 liters per 100 km, which is below the average. It offers great performance, great handling, and it's quite affordable, costing around 500 million VND for a new model. Furthermore, the Yaris is known for being reliable, so you won't have to worry about frequent repairs.

Kia Rio: Another great option for those looking for a fuel-efficient car in Vietnam is the Kia Rio. Like the Yaris, it has a 1.5-liter engine with a fuel economy of 5.8 liters per 100 km. The car also offers a great amount of features and interior space, making it ideal for family trips. Furthermore, it is also relatively affordable, costing at around 470 million VND for a new model.

Hyundai Accent: Another great option for those with a budget of up to 500 million VND is the Hyundai Accent. It has a 1.4-liter engine and has a fuel economy of 5.9 liters per 100 km. It's a great choice for those looking for a reliable car, as it has a great amount of interior space and offers a host of features. Furthermore, the car is available for around 475 million VND, which is perfect for budget-conscious buyers.
