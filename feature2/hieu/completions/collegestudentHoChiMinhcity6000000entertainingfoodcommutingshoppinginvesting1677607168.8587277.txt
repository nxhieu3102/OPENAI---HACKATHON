Entertaining:
- Allocate 20% of budget for entertaining expenses such as going out to movies, eating out, or attending events. 
- Look for discounts and deals to save money on activities. 
- Consider taking advantage of student discounts.

Food: 
- Allocate 40% of budget for food expenses. 
- Take advantage of student discounts on groceries. 
- Look for meal deals and use coupons to save money. 
- Invest in cookware and utensils that are necessary to prepare simple meals. 
- Consider using delivery services as a cost-effective way to enjoy restaurant food.

Commuting: 
- Allocate 20% of budget for commuting expenses. 
- Consider taking public transportation and look for discounts and deals available. 
- Invest in a bicycle and use it for short trips around the city. 
- Consider investing in a motorbike and use it for longer trips.

Shopping: 
- Allocate 10% of budget for shopping expenses. 
- Look for discounts and deals. 
- Consider investing in quality items that will last for a longer period of time. 
- Consider shopping for second-hand items as an alternative to buying new items. 

Investing: 
- Allocate 10% of budget for investing expenses. 
- Research and understand different types of investments. 
- Consider investing in stocks, mutual funds, and other long-term investments to maximize the return on investment. 
- Consider investing in a savings account to build an emergency fund.
