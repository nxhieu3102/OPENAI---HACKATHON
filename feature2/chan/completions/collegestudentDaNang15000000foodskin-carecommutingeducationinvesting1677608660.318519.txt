Food: 8,000,000 VND 

This is the highest cost category and the most essential one. Spend on healthy, nutrient-dense foods that will keep your body energized and functioning optimally. Look for discounts and specials at local markets and grocery stores. Consider preparing meals at home using local ingredients to reduce costs further.

Skin Care: 2,000,000 VND 

Invest in quality skin care products that are tailored to your skin type. Look for natural ingredients whenever possible. Look for specials and discounts to help you save money.

Commuting: 4,000,000 VND

Invest in a reliable motorbike to ensure a safe and efficient journey to and from school. Consider the cost of repair and maintenance when calculating the cost of the motorbike.

Education: 1,000,000 VND

Prioritize learning materials and tools that will help you reach your educational goals. Look for free online resources or discounts on educational materials.

Investing: 500,000 VND

Investing may not be an immediate need, but it is important for long-term financial stability. Think about investing in a diversified portfolio of low-risk investments such as mutual funds and bonds. Research the different types of investments available before investing your money.
