Entertaining: 
Allocate 15% of the budget (600,000 VND) for entertaining, with the majority of this budget going towards coffee trips and small gatherings with friends. Try to take advantage of inexpensive and/or free activities, such as going on a picnic or watching a movie outdoors.

House Renting: 
Allocate 40% of the budget (1,600,000 VND) for house renting. Try to look for a suburban area that is reasonably priced and offers good value for money. Negotiate with the landlord to get the best deal possible.

Education: 
Allocate 20% of the budget (800,000 VND) for education. Focus on investing in TOEIC learning as this will be beneficial for your future career. Consider taking online classes or joining a study group to maximize your budget.

Shopping and Skin-care: 
Allocate 25% of the budget (1,000,000 VND) for shopping and skin-care. Try to be mindful when shopping and only buy what you need. When it comes to skin-care, consider investing in quality products that will last you a long time, while still staying within budget. 

Overall, this spending plan should help you maximize the value of your budget while still meeting your needs. By allocating funds to essential expenses first and focusing on cost-saving measures, you should be able to make the most of your spending.
