Commuting:
For commuting, it is best to invest in a monthly public transport pass to reduce costs and take advantage of discounts. This will provide the most cost-effective option that allows you to travel up to 15 km every day. 

House Renting:
For house renting, it is advisable to look for a house that is not too far from the city center. This will help you save on transportation costs and enable you to easily access other amenities in the city. It is best to look for an affordable house that offers the necessary facilities and amenities within your budget.

Investing:
For investing, you can invest your money into low-risk investments such as mutual funds, bonds, and treasury bills. These investments are often highly liquid, meaning you can easily access your money when needed. Investing in these assets can provide you with a stable source of income and help you build long-term financial stability.
