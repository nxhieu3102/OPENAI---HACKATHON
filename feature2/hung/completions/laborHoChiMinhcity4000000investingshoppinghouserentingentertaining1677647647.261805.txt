Investing: 
Set aside 30% of the total budget for investing. This will help ensure long-term financial stability and help generate more income over time.

Shopping: 
Set aside 10% of the total budget for shopping. This can include buying groceries, clothing, personal care products, and other necessary items.

House Renting: 
Set aside 40% of the total budget for house renting. This should be sufficient to cover rent costs in the city center. Try to negotiate a better deal on rent if possible.

Entertaining: 
Set aside 20% of the total budget for entertaining. This should be enough to cover occasional nights out and eating out. Consider investing in kitchen appliances, such as a rice cooker, to save money on eating out. Investing in a slow cooker or an Instant Pot can also be helpful for cooking at home.
