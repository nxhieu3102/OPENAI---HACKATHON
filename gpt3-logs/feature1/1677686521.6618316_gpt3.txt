==========

1. Rice: For a nutritious meal, why not go with a staple, rice. A 5kg bag of Jasmine or sticky rice costs 75,000 VND, giving you enough energy for the day and easily can be cooked.

3. Cucumber: For an extra crunch, cucumbers can add texture and flavor to your meal. Each cucumber only cost around 5,000 VND depending on the season, and can add a few vitamins and minerals to your diet.

5. Mushrooms: Another good source of proteins, and can be cooked in many forms, from fresh to dried. 550g of mushrooms cost around 40,000 VND.

6. Tofu: For those who are vegetarian, tofu can be a good choice to replace some of the proteins found in meats. 1kg of tofu cost around 25,000 VND.
